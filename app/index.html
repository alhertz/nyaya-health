Nyaya health's average patient in Nepal makes under $200 per year and walks 2.4 hours one-way to get the health care they need.

Health care provides these patients with the dignity and opportunity to break the cycle of poverty.






Nyaya Health delivers comprehensive care to Nepal’s poorest. Since 2008, the organization has delivered care to 137,000 patients, currently employs 160 Nepali staff, and has catalyzed $140,000 of investment from the Nepali government.
